NBA Transactions

Jun 25, 2015 - The Atlanta Hawks selected Kelly Oubre, Jr. in Round 1 with Pick 15 in the 2015 NBA Draft.
Jun 25, 2015 - The draft rights to Kelly Oubre, Jr. was acquired by the Washington Wizards from the Atlanta Hawks in exchange for the draft rights to Jerian Grant, a 2016 2nd round pick (WAS own) and a 2019 2nd round pick (WAS own).
Jul 9, 2015 - Kelly Oubre, Jr. signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards.
Oct 20, 2016 - The Washington Wizards exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Kelly Oubre, Jr..
Oct 21, 2017 - The Washington Wizards exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Kelly Oubre, Jr..
Dec 17, 2018 - Kelly Oubre, Jr. was acquired by the Phoenix Suns, along with Austin Rivers, from the Washington Wizards in exchange for Trevor Ariza.
Jun 25, 2019 - The Phoenix Suns tendered a Qualifying Offer to make Kelly Oubre, Jr. a Restricted FA.
Jul 1, 2019 - Kelly Oubre, Jr., previously with the Phoenix Suns, became a free agent.
Jul 16, 2019 - Kelly Oubre, Jr. signed a multi-year contract with the Phoenix Suns.
Nov 16, 2020 - Kelly Oubre, Jr. was acquired by the Oklahoma City Thunder, along with Ty Jerome, Jalen Lecque, Ricky Rubio, a conditional 2022 1st round pick (PHX own) and a TPE, from the Phoenix Suns in exchange for Abdel Nader, Chris Paul and multiple TPEs.
Nov 22, 2020 - Kelly Oubre, Jr. was acquired by the Golden State Warriors from the Oklahoma City Thunder in exchange for a conditional 2021 1st round pick (GOS own), a 2021 2nd round pick (DEN own), a 2021 2nd round pick (MIN own) and a TPE.
