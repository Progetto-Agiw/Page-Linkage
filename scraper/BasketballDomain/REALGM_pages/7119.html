NBA Transactions

Jun 23, 2011 - The Charlotte Bobcats selected Tobias Harris in Round 1 with Pick 19 in the 2011 NBA Draft.
Jun 23, 2011 - The draft rights to Tobias Harris was acquired by the Milwaukee Bucks, along with Stephen Jackson, Shaun Livingston and Beno Udrih, from the Charlotte Hornets in exchange for Corey Maggette, John Salmons and the draft rights to Jimmer Fredette.
Dec 10, 2011 - Tobias Harris signed a multi-year contract with the Milwaukee Bucks.
Oct 24, 2012 - The Milwaukee Bucks exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Tobias Harris.
Feb 21, 2013 - Tobias Harris was acquired by the Orlando Magic, along with Doron Lamb, Beno Udrih and multiple TPEs, from the Milwaukee Bucks in exchange for Gustavo Ayon, J.J. Redick and Ish Smith.
Oct 26, 2013 - The Orlando Magic exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Tobias Harris.
Jun 30, 2015 - The Orlando Magic tendered a Qualifying Offer to make Tobias Harris a Restricted FA.
Jul 1, 2015 - Tobias Harris, previously with the Orlando Magic, became a free agent.
Jul 14, 2015 - Tobias Harris signed a multi-year contract with the Orlando Magic.
Feb 16, 2016 - Tobias Harris was acquired by the Detroit Pistons from the Orlando Magic in exchange for Ersan Ilyasova and Brandon Jennings.
Jan 29, 2018 - Tobias Harris was acquired by the Los Angeles Clippers, along with Avery Bradley, Boban Marjanovic, a conditional 2018 1st round pick (DET own), a Least Favorable 2019 2nd round pick and multiple TPEs, from the Detroit Pistons in exchange for Blake Griffin, Brice Johnson and Willie Reed.
Feb 6, 2019 - Tobias Harris was acquired by the Philadelphia Sixers, along with Boban Marjanovic and Mike Scott, from the Los Angeles Clippers in exchange for Wilson Chandler, Mike Muscala, Landry Shamet, a conditional 2020 1st round pick (PHL own), a 2021 1st round pick (MIA own), a 2021 2nd round pick (DET own) and a 2023 2nd round pick (DET own).
Jul 1, 2019 - Tobias Harris, previously with the Philadelphia Sixers, became a free agent.
Jul 10, 2019 - Tobias Harris signed a multi-year contract with the Philadelphia Sixers.
