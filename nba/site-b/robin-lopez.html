NBA Transactions

Jun 26, 2008 - The Phoenix Suns selected Robin Lopez in Round 1 with Pick 15 in the 2008 NBA Draft.
Jul 3, 2008 - Robin Lopez signed a multi-year contract with the Phoenix Suns.
Oct 28, 2009 - The Phoenix Suns exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Robin Lopez.
Oct 21, 2010 - The Phoenix Suns exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Robin Lopez.
Jun 28, 2012 - The Phoenix Suns tendered a Qualifying Offer to make Robin Lopez a Restricted FA.
Jul 1, 2012 - Robin Lopez, previously with the Phoenix Suns, became a free agent.
Jul 26, 2012 - Robin Lopez signed a multi-year contract with the Phoenix Suns as part of an eventual sign and trade to New Orleans Hornets.
Jul 27, 2012 - Robin Lopez was acquired by the New Orleans Pelicans, along with Hakim Warrick and cash, from the Phoenix Suns in exchange for Jerome Dyson, Brad Miller, a 2013 2nd round pick (BRK own) and a 2016 2nd round pick (MIN own).
Jul 10, 2013 - Robin Lopez was acquired by the Portland Trail Blazers, along with Terrel Harris, from the New Orleans Pelicans in exchange for the draft rights to Jeff Withey, a conditional 2016 2nd round pick (NYK own), a Swap 2018 2nd round picks and cash.
Jul 1, 2015 - Robin Lopez, previously with the Portland Trail Blazers, became a free agent.
Jul 9, 2015 - The Portland Trail Blazers renounced their free-agent exception rights to Robin Lopez.
Jul 9, 2015 - Robin Lopez signed a multi-year contract with the New York Knicks.
Jun 22, 2016 - Robin Lopez was acquired by the Chicago Bulls, along with Jose Calderon, Jerian Grant and a TPE, from the New York Knicks in exchange for Justin Holiday, Derrick Rose and a 2017 2nd round pick (CHI own).
Jul 1, 2019 - Robin Lopez, previously with the Chicago Bulls, became a free agent.
Jul 6, 2019 - The Chicago Bulls renounced their free-agent exception rights to Robin Lopez.
Jul 12, 2019 - Robin Lopez signed a multi-year contract with the Milwaukee Bucks.
Nov 21, 2020 - Robin Lopez, previously with the Milwaukee Bucks, became a free agent.
Nov 22, 2020 - Robin Lopez signed a contract with the Washington Wizards.
