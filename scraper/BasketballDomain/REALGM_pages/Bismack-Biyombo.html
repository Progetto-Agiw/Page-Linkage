Dec 19, 2011 - Bismack Biyombo signed a multi-year contract with the Charlotte Bobcats.
Oct 26, 2012 - The Charlotte Bobcats exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Bismack Biyombo.
Oct 31, 2013 - The Charlotte Bobcats exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Bismack Biyombo.
Jul 1, 2015 - Bismack Biyombo, previously with the Charlotte Hornets, became a free agent.

Jul 7, 2018 - Bismack Biyombo was acquired by the Charlotte Hornets, along with a Least Favorable 2019 2nd round pick and a Least Favorable 2020 2nd round pick, from the Orlando Magic in exchange for Timofey Mozgov and Julyan Stone.
