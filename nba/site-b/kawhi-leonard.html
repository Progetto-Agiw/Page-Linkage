NBA Transactions

Jun 23, 2011 - The Indiana Pacers selected Kawhi Leonard in Round 1 with Pick 15 in the 2011 NBA Draft.
Jun 23, 2011 - The draft rights to Kawhi Leonard was acquired by the San Antonio Spurs, along with the draft rights to Davis Bertans, the draft rights to Erazem Lorbek and a TPE, from the Indiana Pacers in exchange for George Hill.
Dec 13, 2011 - Kawhi Leonard signed a multi-year contract with the San Antonio Spurs.
Oct 26, 2012 - The San Antonio Spurs exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Kawhi Leonard.
Dec 19, 2012 - The San Antonio Spurs assigned Kawhi Leonard to the Austin Spurs of the G League.
Dec 20, 2012 - The San Antonio Spurs recalled Kawhi Leonard from the Austin Spurs of the G League.
Oct 28, 2013 - The San Antonio Spurs exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Kawhi Leonard.
Jun 30, 2015 - The San Antonio Spurs tendered a Qualifying Offer to make Kawhi Leonard a Restricted FA.
Jul 1, 2015 - Kawhi Leonard, previously with the San Antonio Spurs, became a free agent.
Jul 16, 2015 - Kawhi Leonard signed a multi-year contract with the San Antonio Spurs.
Jul 18, 2018 - Kawhi Leonard was acquired by the Toronto Raptors, along with Danny Green, cash and a TPE, from the San Antonio Spurs in exchange for DeMar DeRozan, Jakob Poeltl and a conditional 2019 1st round pick (TOR own).
Jul 1, 2019 - Kawhi Leonard, previously with the Toronto Raptors, became a free agent.
Jul 9, 2019 - Kawhi Leonard signed a multi-year contract with the Los Angeles Clippers.
