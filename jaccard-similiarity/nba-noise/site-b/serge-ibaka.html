NBA Transactions

Jun 26, 2008 - The Seattle SuperSonics selected Serge Ibaka in Round 1 with Pick 24 in the 2008 NBA Draft.
Jul 6, 2009 - Serge Ibaka signed a multi-year contract with the Oklahoma City Thunder.
Oct 25, 2010 - The Oklahoma City Thunder exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Serge Ibaka.
Jun 27, 2011 - The Oklahoma City Thunder exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Serge Ibaka.
Aug 18, 2012 - Serge Ibaka signed a rookie scale extension with the Oklahoma City Thunder.
Jun 23, 2016 - Serge Ibaka was acquired by the Orlando Magic from the Oklahoma City Thunder in exchange for Ersan Ilyasova, Victor Oladipo and the draft rights to Domantas Sabonis.
Feb 14, 2017 - Serge Ibaka was acquired by the Toronto Raptors from the Orlando Magic in exchange for Terrence Ross and a Least Favorable 2017 1st round pick.
Jul 1, 2017 - Serge Ibaka, previously with the Toronto Raptors, became a free agent.
Jul 7, 2017 - Serge Ibaka signed a multi-year contract with the Toronto Raptors.
Nov 21, 2020 - Serge Ibaka, previously with the Toronto Raptors, became a free agent.
Nov 25, 2020 - Serge Ibaka signed a multi-year contract with the Los Angeles Clippers.
